ABOUT US

SSCPL STARTED ITS JOURNEY AS A PARTNERSHIP FIRM M/S. SAGAR INTERNATIONAL IN 1974. WE GOT INCORPORATED AS A PRIVATE LIMITED COMPANY IN 1999.

As M/s. Sagar Speciality Chemicals Private Limited.

SSCPL has always been a reliable and trusted partner to domestic and export market in supply chain management. In 2016, we explored and found a niche market for contract manufacturing. 

With the support of well-known multinational brands, SSCPL has become a preferred supplier of niche performance chemical to various industrial segments.

COMPANY POLICY

  • We deal only with branded, recognized and quality products. We do not deal with inferior quality, adulterated or unrecognized products.
  • SSCPL does not promote any corruption or bribery while dealing with any of our customers or government authorities.
  • SSCPL strictly adheres to Child Labor Act and Sexual Harassment Policy. We believe in providing a free and fair work environment to our employees.
  • SSCPL adheres to Safety, Health & Environmental policies and engages with principals following the Safety, Health & Environmental policies.
  • SSCPL adheres to all the company norms as laid down by the UN as well as the Government of India.

MANAGEMENT TEAM

Ravindra Laxmidas Sampat - Founder & Director.

A visionary leader who started the company and responsible to take SSCPL to new heights. He has completed his graduation in Commerce and with an enriching experience of 50+ years, he ensured SSCPL has established a strong position in speciality chemical market.

Hardik Ravindra Sampat – Managing Director.

A chemical Engineer (B.E. Chemicals) with MBA in Marketing, he started his career with IOTL (Indian Oil Tanking Limited). He joined SSCPL in 2012 and with backing of his technical knowledge and experience of 2+ years, he introduced a new line of business into contract manufacturing and customized chemical solutions. He currently heads technical aspect of product development ensuring diversification of business.

Jaydeep Kantilal Panchal - Business Development Manager

A chemical engineer (B. E. Chemicals) with diploma in French language (DELF Al), he started his career in 2011 with marketing of speciality chemicals. He joined SSCPL in 2018 and since then has been responsible for developing new business and market for SSCPL. He jointly works with the Director with planning and strategies to ensure business growth.

Mehul Chandulal Dattani - Head of Operations

Mehul holds a diploma in Export & Import Management and with 17+ years of experience in multiple profiles, he takes care of entire operations right from order management to delivery of products. He jointly works with the Management Team and responsible for logistics and documentation process.

Brijesh Shah

Brijesh Shah is engineer with MBA in International Business Management and 20 years of experience of strong well-rounded track record in a competitive international market. He is responsible to ensure Sagar Speciality Chemicals Private Limited establishes a strong presence in international market.
